Ah, you thought you were heading towards something interesting, didn’t you? But instead, you’ve found yourself in the classic internet trap known as the Rickroll! What is a Rickroll, you ask? It’s a prank involving an unexpected appearance of the music video for Rick Astley’s 1987 hit “Never Gonna Give You Up.” This meme has been the gift that keeps on giving, bringing laughter and a bit of nostalgia to millions across the globe.

The Rickroll has become a cultural phenomenon, transcending generations and continuing to surprise the uninitiated.
